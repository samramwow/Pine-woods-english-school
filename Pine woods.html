Our Curriculum

We offer comprehensive education divided into three core stages, ensuring age-appropriate learning and development from early childhood through high school.

  • Pre-Primary (Nursery, LKG, UKG): Focus on play-way methods, basic motor skills, and foundational literacy.
  • Primary & Middle School (1st to 8th Grade): Strong emphasis on core subjects, critical thinking, sports, and co-curricular activities.
  • High School (9th & 10th Grade): Advanced academics, board exam preparation, career counseling, and leadership building.
