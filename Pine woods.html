About Us

Welcome to Pine Woods English School. We are dedicated to building a strong community of learners, educators, and parents. Below are some frequently asked questions about our institution.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school timings?

Our regular academic hours are from 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM, Monday through Friday. Extracurricular activities usually take place between 2:30 PM and 4:00 PM.

Do you provide transportation facilities?

Yes, we operate a fleet of safe and secure buses that cover major routes across the city. Bus tracking is also available via the Pine Woods App.

What extracurricular activities are offered?

We believe in all-round development. Students can participate in sports (basketball, cricket, athletics), arts and crafts, coding clubs, and debate societies.

How can parents track student progress?

Parents can download the Pine Woods App from the portal. It provides real-time updates on attendance, exam grades, assignments, and direct communication with teachers.
